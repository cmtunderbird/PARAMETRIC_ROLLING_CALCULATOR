fix #10: launch.bat now kills only its own PIDs, not all node.exe processes

Previously used taskkill /F /IM node.exe which silently killed VS Code,
other dev servers, Discord, and any other Node-based tool on the machine.

Fix: track the specific PIDs of the CMEMS proxy and Vite processes started
by the bat file. On exit, only those two PIDs are terminated.
Message confirms: "Other Node processes on your machine were not affected."
