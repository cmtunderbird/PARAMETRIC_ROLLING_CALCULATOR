fix: launch.bat - kill stale processes, single browser, no duplicate Vite

Three bugs fixed:
1. Stale node processes from previous sessions occupied ports 3001-3004,
   causing Vite to bind to unexpected ports (visible as "localhost:3003").
   Fix: taskkill /F /IM node.exe at startup before launching anything.

2. launch.bat started Vite in background AND ran "npm run dev" again at the
   bottom as a foreground keepalive — two Vite instances, two browser windows.
   Fix: removed the second "npm run dev". Window now stays open with "pause".

3. Browser now opens ONCE after detecting the correct port (3000-3005 scan).

Cleanup on exit: pressing any key kills all node.exe processes cleanly.
